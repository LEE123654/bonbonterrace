Our Story

On the terrace

Whether it's a quiet meal, a family breakfast, or dinner with friends, we keep tables both inside and out on our terrace — a favorite spot for groups and couples alike near Bukhara's old town.

Our kitchen is best known for Belgian and Viennese waffles, fresh cakes and cheesecakes, hearty salads, and a pot of house pomegranate tea.
